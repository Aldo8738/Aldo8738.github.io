Process

1. Strategy

In the strategy stage I align with the stakeholders the main information that will be represented in the application or website

  • Objectives
  • User Group
  • Demographics
  • Voice
  • Needs and Audiences
  • Competition Research

2. Outline of Scope

Devising the content and functionality requierments. Creating Personas to understand better the needs of the User

Content
  • Text
  • Images
  • Audio
  • Video
  • Files
  • etc.
Functionality
  • Task based systems that process user input

3. Sitemap

Defining the Information Architecture, in other words, categorize, organize and label the information. Settling the hierarchy and sequences for the user to follow

Sample

4. Wireframes

Creating simplified and low detail mockups. Creating the skeleton of the application or website

5. Mockups and prototype

Creating a highly detailed design to give a glimps on how the app will look and work

6. Coding

Here is where the magic happens. Translating the design into code & adding all the necessary content and animations
